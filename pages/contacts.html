ABOUT US

Archetyp Works is an independent game studio dedicated to crafting narrative-driven action-adventure experiences. Our team blends classic storytelling with modern gameplay systems to build immersive worlds, memorable characters, and thrilling challenges. BellFall is our passion project — a tribute to bold heroes, inventive mechanics, and striking visual design.

We value community feedback and collaboration. If you're interested in playtesting, contributing art, music, or code, or just want to follow our progress, reach out via the contacts above. Join Archetyp Works and help shape the next chapter of BellFall.
